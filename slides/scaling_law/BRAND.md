# Brand Notes

This template uses public visual identity resources from the Peking University Visual Identity Management Office:

https://vim.pku.edu.cn/xzzq/index.htm

Included assets:

- `public/brand/pku-emblem-red.png`
- `public/brand/pku-emblem-white.png`
- `public/brand/pku-lockup-horizontal.png`

The primary color follows the official basic specification:

- Beida Red: `#94070A`

Before public or formal use, read the official basic usage guide and misuse guide from the download page. Do not redraw, stretch, outline, recolor, or crop the emblem. Keep sufficient clear space around the emblem and wordmark.
